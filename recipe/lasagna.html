Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with a hearty meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheese. To start, a rich meat sauce is prepared by cooking ground beef with onions, garlic, and tomato sauce, seasoned with herbs like oregano and basil. While the sauce simmers, a creamy béchamel sauce is made by whisking butter, flour, and milk until smooth. The lasagna is assembled by layering the pasta sheets, meat sauce, béchamel, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish. It is then baked until golden and bubbly, resulting in a flavorful, comforting meal perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • tomato sauce
  • sugar
  • Spice and seasoning
  • lasagna noodles
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
