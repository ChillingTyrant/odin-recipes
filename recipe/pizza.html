Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish featuring a crispy dough base topped with a variety of ingredients, including tomato sauce, cheese, and your choice of meats or vegetables. To make a traditional pizza, you begin by preparing the dough, which is rolled out and topped with a flavorful tomato sauce. Mozzarella cheese is generously spread on top, and then the pizza is customized with toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, or fresh basil. Itâ€™s baked in a hot oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted, creating a delicious and versatile dish perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni (optional)
  • Mushrooms (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough and let it rise
  2. Roll out the dough into a round shape
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough
  4. Add mozzarella cheese and chosen toppings
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at high temperature
  6. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot
